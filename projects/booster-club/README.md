# Eastlake Boys Basketball Booster Club

_Established March 2026_

## Overview

The Eastlake Boys Basketball Booster Club is a non-profit organization supporting the Eastlake High School (EHS) Boys Basketball Program in Chula Vista, CA. The club involves the community and families of all basketball players to fund and provide special services that enhance the experience for players, coaches, and fans.

- **Stage:** Inaugural year — organizing and establishing

## **Summary**

### **Basketball Booster Club Establishment Meeting**

The meeting focused on discussing the establishment of a basketball booster club, with participants introducing themselves and their sons who played on various teams. Coach Gardocki provided an overview of the club's goals, emphasizing that fundraising would primarily support off-season activities, including tournaments at San Diego State and Montgomery, with potential trips to Arizona, LA, and Las Vegas. The group agreed to review and revise the draft bylaws, with Tony volunteering to take notes and share the document during the meeting.

### **Basketball Booster Club Planning Meeting**

The group discussed plans for establishing a Booster Club for the basketball program, focusing on hosting tournaments and fundraising activities. Ryan shared that tournaments typically begin in June with events like the San Diego State Tournament, and suggested hosting more tournaments in the South Bay to generate revenue. The team acknowledged the need to form a non-profit organization and create bylaws before moving forward with fundraising efforts, with plans to model the process after the football program's experience. Next steps include finalizing the bylaws and selecting board members before submitting paperwork for non-profit status.

### **Booster Club Bylaws Review**

The group reviewed and discussed edits to the booster club bylaws, focusing on clarifying the mission statement and purpose in Article 2\. Karla suggested adding specific details about supporting off-season tournaments and activities, while emphasizing that the booster club should not interfere with coaching decisions. The team agreed to restructure the mission statement into two paragraphs, with the first paragraph serving as an overarching mission and the second paragraph detailing specific purposes. They also discussed the need to clarify the distinction between the executive board and general membership. The conversation ended with a note about fundraising activities requiring pre-approval from Eastlake High School's Assistant Principal and Student Activities, with Coach Gardocki confirming this requirement applies to off-season activities as well.

### **Eastlake Basketball Booster Bylaws Finalization**

The group discussed and made progress on finalizing the bylaws for the Eastlake Boys Basketball Booster Club. They agreed to open nominations to all interested parents rather than just the current meeting participants, and decided to finalize the bylaws at their next meeting before proceeding with nominations and elections. Key decisions included aligning the organizational year with the school year (July 1 to June 30), clarifying officer signatures on documents, and specifying that meeting attendance should be by "should meet or be present" rather than requiring mandatory attendance. The group also discussed the need to establish a website and determine how to handle voting processes, potentially using Zoom's polling features for anonymous voting.

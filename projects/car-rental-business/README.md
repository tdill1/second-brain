# Car Rental Business

(Formerly referenced as "Budget Poway")

## Status
Active — Revenue-generating operations

## Description
A car rental business operating in the Poway / San Diego area. The operation manages a fleet of vehicles, handles bookings and customer service, and drives revenue through daily and weekly rentals. Current focus is on stabilizing operations, improving fleet utilization, and increasing profitability through better pricing and reduced downtime.

## Current Priorities
1. **Maximize fleet utilization rate** — Target 75%+ utilization across all vehicles by reducing turnaround time and filling gap days with short-term rentals
2. **Streamline booking and payment workflow** — Reduce manual steps in reservations, contracts, and payment collection to save time and minimize errors
3. **Control maintenance and insurance costs** — Implement a preventive maintenance schedule and review insurance coverage to avoid surprise expenses eating into margins

## Active Workstreams

### A: Fleet Management & Maintenance
- Maintain a vehicle status tracker (available, rented, in maintenance, out of service)
- Schedule preventive maintenance at mileage intervals, not reactively
- Evaluate fleet composition — identify underperforming vehicles for replacement or removal

### B: Revenue & Pricing Optimization
- Review pricing against local competitors monthly (Turo, Enterprise, local independents)
- Implement dynamic pricing for peak periods (holidays, weekends, events)
- Track revenue per vehicle per month to identify top and bottom performers

### C: Customer Experience & Operations
- Standardize pickup/drop-off process with a checklist
- Collect post-rental feedback (simple text-based survey)
- Maintain a clean, professional presentation for every vehicle at handoff

### D: Accounting & Compliance
- Reconcile income and expenses weekly
- Ensure all vehicles carry current registration, insurance, and inspection
- Track tax obligations (sales tax on rentals, depreciation for fleet assets)

## Next 3 Actions
1. **Build a fleet dashboard** — Create a simple spreadsheet or doc tracking each vehicle's status, next maintenance date, and month-to-date revenue. Save to `car-rental-business/fleet-tracker.md`
2. **Benchmark pricing** — Check rates for 3 local competitors and Turo for comparable vehicles; adjust pricing if there's more than a 10% gap
3. **Set up a monthly P&L review** — Document income, fuel, maintenance, insurance, and platform fees for the current month to establish a baseline

## Definition of Done
- Fleet utilization consistently at or above 75%
- Monthly P&L is positive and tracked in a repeatable format
- Booking-to-handoff process documented and takes under 15 minutes of active work per rental
- All vehicles current on maintenance, registration, and insurance with no lapses

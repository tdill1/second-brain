# TrueSentiments

## Status
Active — Competitive research and strategic planning phase

## Description
TrueSentiments (truesentiments.com) is a wellness and self-care brand co-founded by Beverly and Tony Dill, offering products like the MyGratiBox interactive self-care kit alongside mindfulness content. This project tracks competitive research, brand analysis, and strategic planning related to the TrueSentiments business — including site performance, product positioning, content strategy, and growth opportunities in the wellness/gratitude market.

## Current Priorities
1. **Complete the competitive and brand audit** — Synthesize the site scrapes, blog analysis, and deep-context annotations from Feb 2025 into actionable insights about positioning, gaps, and opportunities
2. **Identify the highest-leverage growth channel** — Evaluate whether the biggest opportunity is in e-commerce conversion optimization, content/SEO, social media, email marketing, or wholesale/partnerships
3. **Define the 90-day strategic roadmap** — Translate research findings into a prioritized action plan with clear owners and milestones

## Active Workstreams

### A: Research & Analysis
- Site architecture and UX review (based on site scrape data)
- Blog content audit — assess quality, SEO potential, posting cadence, and engagement
- Competitive landscape — identify 3–5 direct competitors in the self-care kit / gratitude box space and compare pricing, positioning, and content strategy
- Document all findings in `02-research/`

### B: Brand & Product Strategy
- Evaluate current product lineup (MyGratiBox, journals, digital offerings) for margin and demand
- Assess brand voice consistency across site, blog, and social channels
- Identify potential new products or bundles based on audience needs and competitor gaps

### C: Digital Marketing & Growth
- Audit current SEO performance (top keywords, organic traffic trends, backlink profile)
- Evaluate email marketing setup (list size, flows, open rates) and recommend quick wins
- Review social media presence across Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest, YouTube, and Twitter for engagement and growth trends

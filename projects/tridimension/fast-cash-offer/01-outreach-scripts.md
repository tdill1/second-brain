# Outreach Scripts — "Never Miss Another Customer" Offer

---

## SCRIPT 1: Cold Call / Warm Call (Phone)

### The "Secret Shopper" Opener
*Before calling, actually call their business and let it ring. If they don't answer or it goes to voicemail, you now have proof.*

> "Hey [Name], this is Tony with TriDimension. I'm going to be real with you — I called your business about 20 minutes ago as a potential customer and nobody picked up. No text back, no nothing. 
> 
> I'm not saying that to be critical — I know you're busy running your business. But here's the thing: I build automated systems that catch those calls and instantly text the customer back so you never lose them. 
> 
> If I could show you how that works in about 10 minutes, would that be worth a quick look?"

### If They DID Answer Earlier:
> "Hey [Name], this is Tony with TriDimension. I actually called your shop earlier and [you/your team] picked up — that's great. But here's my question: what happens at 7pm when you're done for the day and someone calls? Or on the weekend?
>
> I set up systems for businesses like yours that automatically reply to missed calls, book appointments, and get you more Google reviews on autopilot. Takes me about 3 days to install. Can I show you how it works?"

### Handling Objections:

**"I don't have the budget right now"**
> "I totally get that. Let me ask you this — how many calls do you think you miss per week? Even if it's 5, and even one of those would have been a $300 job, that's $1,200/month walking out the door. The system pays for itself in the first week."

**"I already have something for that"**
> "Nice — what are you using? [Listen] Got it. Does it also handle your Google reviews, book appointments, and put all your messages in one inbox? Because that's usually where the gaps are."

**"Send me some info"**
> "Absolutely. What's the best email? And just so I send you the right stuff — are you mainly losing leads from missed calls, or is it more of a reviews/online visibility thing?"

## SCRIPT 4: Google Maps Prospecting Method

### Step-by-Step:
1. Open Google Maps
2. Search "[niche] near [city]" (e.g., "plumber near Chula Vista")
3. Look for businesses with:
   - Under 50 Google reviews (need review automation)
   - No website or a bad website (need webchat)
   - No "Message" button on their Google listing
4. Call them at 6pm on a weekday (after hours test)
5. If no answer → they're a PERFECT prospect
6. Use the Secret Shopper phone script the next day
7. Log everything in a spreadsheet or Notion database

### What to Track:
| Business Name | Phone | Answered? | Reviews | Website? | Notes | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ABC Plumbing | 619-555-0123 | No | 12 reviews | Yes - no chat | Called 6pm, VM | To contact |

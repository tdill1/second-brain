# abstractwishes

## Status
Active — Brand building phase

## Description
abstractwishes is a social media brand focused on creative content and community engagement. The project centers on developing a distinct visual identity, growing an organic following, and establishing a consistent content pipeline across social platforms. Long-term goal is to build an audience-first brand that can be monetized through partnerships, merch, or digital products.

## Current Priorities
1. **Define brand identity and visual language** — Lock in color palette, typography, tone of voice, and content pillars so all output is cohesive
2. **Establish a sustainable content calendar** — Build a repeatable weekly posting cadence (minimum 3x/week) with batched creation sessions
3. **Grow to first 1,000 engaged followers** — Focus on one primary platform, optimize posting times, and engage daily in niche communities

## Active Workstreams

### A: Brand Identity & Guidelines
- Finalize logo, color palette, and font stack
- Write a one-page brand voice guide (tone, dos/don'ts, sample captions)
- Create 5–10 reusable content templates (Canva or Figma)

### B: Content Production
- Identify 3–4 content pillars (e.g., inspiration, behind-the-scenes, community prompts, curated shares)
- Batch-produce two weeks of posts as a buffer
- Test short-form video (Reels/TikTok) vs. static posts for engagement

### C: Community & Growth
- Identify 20 accounts in adjacent niches for engagement and collaboration
- Respond to every comment and DM within 24 hours
- Track follower growth and engagement rate weekly

## Next 3 Actions
1. **Draft the brand guide** — One page covering voice, visual identity, and content pillars. Save to `abstractwishes/brand-guide.md`
2. **Create first content batch** — Design and schedule 6 posts (2 weeks at 3x/week) using the brand templates
3. **Audit competitor accounts** — Review 5 similar brands, note what's working (format, hooks, hashtags), and document takeaways in `abstractwishes/competitive-notes.md`

## Definition of Done
- Brand guide documented and applied consistently across all posts
- Content calendar running with at least a one-week buffer at all times
- 1,000 followers on primary platform with engagement rate above 3%
- Clear path to monetization identified (even if not yet activated)

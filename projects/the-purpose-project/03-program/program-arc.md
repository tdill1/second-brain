# Program Arc (Draft)

## 0) Intake & trust-building
- Eligibility, barriers, logistics
- Participant goals and constraints
- Baseline measures

## 1) Self-discovery (Purpose Foundations)
- Values clarification
- Strengths and energizers
- Identity narratives (what have you overcome / what do you care about?)
- Social context (family expectations, community needs)

Deliverable: **Personal Purpose Statement** (draft) + evidence (stories, examples)

## 2) Translation (Purpose → Plan)
- “Purpose themes” → roles/paths mapping
- Skill gap analysis
- Shortlist of 2–3 viable pathways

Deliverable: **90-day plan** + training path + support plan

## 3) Skill-building (Training)
- Core skills: communication, collaboration, basic digital skills
- Track skills: varies by pathway (IT support, data, healthcare, trades, etc.)

Deliverable: portfolio/credential(s)

## 4) Placement (Work)
- Resume/portfolio, interview practice
- Employer matching + warm introductions
- Paid internship / apprenticeship / entry role

Deliverable: job placement + onboarding plan

## 5) Retention (Mentorship)
- 90-day retention support
- Peer community + mentor check-ins

Deliverable: retention milestones, progression plan

## Measurement & learning
- Pre/post surveys
- Attendance/completion
- Placement and retention
- Qualitative interviews

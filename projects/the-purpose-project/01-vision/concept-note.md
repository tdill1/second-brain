# Concept Note (Draft)

## Problem
Many young people lack a clear, lived sense of purpose and a practical pathway to turn strengths/interests into sustainable work. This can lead to disengagement in school/training, underemployment, and poor wellbeing.

## Idea
Create a structured, supportive program that helps participants:
1) uncover strengths, values, and motivators (self-discovery),
2) translate insights into an actionable plan (goal design + coaching),
3) gain market-relevant skills (education/training), and
4) secure paid work (job placement) aligned with their purpose.

## What makes it different
- Evidence-based self-discovery + coaching (not just “career quizzes”)
- A clear bridge from purpose → skills → employment
- Responsible AI as an assistive tool (reflection prompts, planning, practice, matching)
- Mentorship + employer partnerships to reduce drop-off

## Target population (to decide)
- Example: ages 16–24, first-generation students, under-resourced communities, or youth transitioning out of foster care / justice involvement.

## Outcomes (examples)
- Purpose clarity score improvement
- Training completion rate
- Job placement rate within 90 days
- Job retention at 6 and 12 months
- Wellbeing and self-efficacy improvements

## Pilot (example)
- 10–25 participant cohort
- 8–12 weeks
- Hybrid delivery
- Wraparound support + mentorship

## Funding request (placeholder)
Support a pilot cohort, evaluation, and the build-out of an employer placement pipeline.

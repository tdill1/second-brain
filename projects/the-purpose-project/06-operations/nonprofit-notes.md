# Nonprofit / Operations Notes (Draft)

Not legal advice.

## Entity questions
- Start as a project under fiscal sponsorship vs forming a new 501(c)(3)
- State of incorporation
- Board formation
- Conflict of interest policy

## Compliance considerations
- Working with minors (if <18): consent, safeguarding, background checks
- Data privacy: participant data handling, retention policy
- Employment partnerships: fair hiring, non-discrimination

## Evaluation
- Define metrics early
- Consider an external evaluator for credibility

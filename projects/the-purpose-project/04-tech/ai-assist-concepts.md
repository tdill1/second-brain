# Responsible AI Assist Concepts (Draft)

Goal: Use AI to *augment* reflection, planning, practice, and matching—never replace human judgment or care.

## Potential features
- Guided reflection prompts + journaling structure
- Summarization of participant narratives into themes (values/strengths)
- Goal translation: purpose themes → feasible pathways
- Practice: interview role-play, communication scenarios
- Resource navigator: training options, scholarships, community support
- Job matching: skills/constraints → opportunity shortlist (with transparency)

## Guardrails
- Human-in-the-loop for any high-stakes decisions
- Clear consent and data minimization
- Avoid labeling/diagnosis
- Bias testing and monitoring
- Explainability: why a suggestion was made

## Data policy (initial stance)
- Collect the minimum needed
- Participant owns their personal narrative
- Default: do not use participant data to train models

# Donor Pitch Outline (Draft)

## 1) Hook
A clear sense of purpose is a protective factor—and a practical compass for education and work.

## 2) The gap
Youth programs often address either:
- personal development, **or**
- training and placement,
…but not the full bridge from identity → skills → employment.

## 3) The solution
A cohort-based program that combines:
- self-discovery + coaching
- evidence-based tools
- responsible AI support
- employer partnerships for placement

## 4) Why now
- Youth mental health and disconnection are rising
- Skills-based hiring is expanding
- AI can lower the cost of personalized support (with human oversight)

## 5) What you fund
- Participant scholarships/support (transport, devices)
- Coaches/mentors
- Training partnerships
- Employer outreach + placement staff
- Evaluation and learning

## 6) Proof plan
Pilot → evaluate → iterate → scale.

## 7) The ask
- Amount: TBD
- Use of funds: pilot cohort + evaluation + employer pipeline
- Timeline: 6–12 months

## 8) Close
This is an opportunity to fund *purpose-aligned pathways*—measurable outcomes with human dignity at the center.
